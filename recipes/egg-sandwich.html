Egg Sandwich Recipe

Home

Description

The simplest yet creamiest egg sandwich recipe you can do at home.

Ingredients

Servings: 2

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 2 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Put in the eggs inside the boiling water for 6 minutes.
  3. Once boiled, put the eggs inside iced water right away.
  4. Peel the eggs and seperate the yolks and put into a seperate bowl.
  5. Mix maiyonnase and the yolks until it becomes a paste consistency.
  6. Mix the yolk paste with the egg whites until combined.
  7. Add salt to taste and pepper and continue mixing well.
  8. Spread the eggs onto two slices of bread and sandwich it with the other two breads.
