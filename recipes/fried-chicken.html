Fried Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

The crispiest fried chicken with the simplest ingredients.

Ingredients

Servings: 1

  • 200g chicken thigh
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Beat the egg in a small bowl until smooth.
  3. Mix the flour with a pinch of salt and pepper in a shallow plate.
  4. Coat the chicken throughly in the egg.
  5. Toss the egg-washed chicken in the flour until fully covered.
  6. Heat enough oil in a small pan in medium heat.
  7. Fry the chicken for around 8 to 9 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Drain the chicken on a paper towel before serving.
