Pizza Dough Recipe

Home

Description

The simplest pizza dough recipe you can do at home.

Ingredients

Servings: 1

  • 100g water
  • 150g bread flour
  • 0.1g instant yeast
  • 3.5g salt

Steps

  1. Mix water and yeast and pour it into 150g of bread flour until a shaggy dough ball is form.
  2. Add all the salt and continue mixing.
  3. Once a dough ball is formed, let it rest for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  4. Do 3 sets of stretch and fold and let ir rest 30 minutes each time.
  5. Put the dough in an airtight container and put it in the refrigerator.
  6. Let it rise for 24 hours and put it out at room temperature for an hour before baking.
